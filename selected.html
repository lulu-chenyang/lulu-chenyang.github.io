Stay Well Selected is a concept design based on Delos Labs' product evaluation and inspired by a business opportunity which has the potential to commercialized the product evaluation product.

Product evaluation is based on scientific research and industry best practices, Delos Labs conducts proprietary product review and testing based on rigorous criteria on material safety, functionality, marketing claims, and user experience. We then help customers choose the “best fit” products and how to use them appropriately and safely for different use cases.

Unique Challenges

Delos helps products with real health & wellness benefit to stand out by systematic scientific evaluation. More and more manufactures send their product to us for product evaluation, home owners and consumers reached out Delos for product recommendation. We sensed the market need and seek solutions to help customers to make better purchasing decision while helping manufacture sell products that have a good impact on human health and well-being.

Working Process

The most time consuming process in users' purchasing process is the information search and evaluate alternative process. From our customer's perspected, they would like to eliminate their user's hesitation. For product user, they are looking for the best product for themselves and for their family. Stay Well Selected is designed as a product to help both Delos customers and the products user.
