Stay Well is a program created for healthy travel experiences. Collaborated with famous hotel brands like MGM, Marriott, Wyndham, etc., Stay Well provides personalized lighting, jetlag relief, purified air, etc. to minimize the impact that travel has on humans.

I've been helping Stay Well team to evaluate the existing product, streamline the product evaluation process, update the website and Stay Well Shope website, build the product roadmap, and prototype new products.
