About Me

Working in a multidisciplinary team for 5 years, I played multiple roles in the UX world: I worked as a UX designer, product designer, UX researcher, data analyst, and sometimes product manager. I also coded, making apps, websites, and animations.

Trained as an architect, I've been exploring how to balance "human comfort" and "sustainability" to make a better world. In the UX term, "usability" and "simplicity". When I design buildings, I used 3D models and building performance simulation. Now, as a UX designer, I organize workshops, make prototypes, and conduct user studies.

What's more about me?

I used to manage an international team to design and build labs. I supervised students, interns, junior members who are now playing different roles in different companies all over the world.
