The Unique Challenge

When software meets hardware, our team many challenge are facing. The feedback from software and hardware can be given to the user at different time and space.

The impact of wellness is not be visible by the users in the short-term

One of the callenges is how to “Make the air quality data accessible and empowering”

Customer got data but not information and they usually ignore the data very soon.

  • We are athlete and referee at the same time:
  • There is a trade-off between the amount of information that can be displayed (expressiveness) and the degree to which the specified goals can be achieved (effectiveness).
  • Does majority of users want to be more engaged or automated? Have more information or less details? Know anything specifically?
