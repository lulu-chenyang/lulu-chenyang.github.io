The IoT solution for hositality

Ketra Light and its lighting control system used to be an important part of Stay Well. However, after acquired by Lutron Electronics in April 2018, Ketra was no longer able to support the business of Stay Well. At the same time, the newly-developed wellness algorithm requires a smarter platform to be deployed. Therefore, the product team decided to design our lighting control system and go beyond Ketra controller offers.

Ketra Lighting Controller

Challenges

The project was initiated by the Stay Well team, while the research and design are conducted in the lab team, and the venture team communicates with the manufacture and the lighting company. Also, besides the original function, the Moodbox needs to be prepared for far more significant ancillary device controls through the cloud in the long term.

Discover

A series of workshops were organized to understand the users and users' needs.

Hotel Guest

45% of the visitors came to resort for pleasure. Their average stay is 3.4 nights to 4.4 days. They need to be able to control the lights, set, and enjoy the Dawn Simulation through the Moodbox.

Room service

Cleaning staffs need to rest the device every time after a guest checks out. They need a simple and easy operation which doesn't cost extra workload. The operation needs to be written in the cleaning protocol.

Commissioning team

Currently, the commissioning work is majorly done by Stay Well team. Potentially, in the future, when the business gets expand, a 3rd-party commissioning team will conduct the job.

Goal

Design a light controller simple enough for hotel guests, room service staff, and commissioning team.

Working Process

Wireframe

Hotel Guest

Hotel guess interface was developed based on existing Ketra capability and the new ideas generated in the stakeholder meeting. The button and slider for Circadian Experience, Energize Experience, Relax Experience, Play Experience, and their dim slider maintained the same. While users are able to set a Dawn Simulation and use it through the Moodbox. It was quickly shipped to the factory along with a purposed data infrastructure map to evaluate the feasibility.

Hardware Design

3D Print

While the venture team worked with factors on building the foundational elements for communication, storage, processing power, etc, My team designed the hardware of the device and drafted the commissioning document with technology and commissioning teams. In addition to sketches, 3D printing technology was used to polish the detail of the hardware.

Prototype

Multiple prototypes were developed during the development process. The "Set Alarm" button is replaced by a more accurate description "Set Dawn Simulation". The Stay Well blue is used for the focus button to improve brand integrity.

Users are able to set the time, set repeat, and select sound track. Once set, the user will experience natrual wake up 10 minutes before the set wake-up time.
